<p> RULES</p>
<li class="list-group-item">A competitive game consists of
 equal 30-minute halves with a 10-15 minute break.</li>
 <li class="list-group-item">A team cannot keep possession
 of the ball without attempting to attack.</li>
 <li class="list-group-item">The start is awarded to the 
 team that wins the coin toss.</li>
<li class="list-group-item"> A match begins with both
 teams in their own half.</li>

<li class="list-group-item"> A goal can be scored from
 any type of throw.</li>

<li class="list-group-item"> A player can run with the
 ball for three steps maximum.</li>
<li class="list-group-item"> A player can hold a ball for
 up to three seconds maximum.</li>

<li class="list-group-item"> A player can continuously 
dribble, providing they bounce the ball.</li>

<li class="list-group-item"> A player can take three 
steps maximum before and after dribbling (no 'double 
dribble').</li>
